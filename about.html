About Blood Group

1)ABO System

2)Rhesus System

the ABO System is divided into four groups.They are O, A, B, AB Groups.In this "O-ve" blood group persons can donate blood to all people irrespective of blood group and "AB+ve" can take the blood from any blood group.All other groups have some limitations with their recieving blood group.
